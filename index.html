// about me

Researcher & engineer

I'm a Ph.D. student in Electrical Engineering at the University of Central Florida, focused on scientific machine learning for dynamical systems. My research combines physics priors with modern deep learning to build models that generalize better and control systems more reliably.

Previously, I completed my M.S. at Northern Arizona University (Thesis: Physics-Informed ML for HVAC Systems) and my B.S. at Hanoi University of Science and Technology (Thesis: Fuzzy-Based Traction Control for Electric Vehicles).

Outside the lab, I run a Research Talk YouTube series for Vietnamese engineering researchers worldwide — 6,500+ subscribers and 30,000+ hours of watch time.
